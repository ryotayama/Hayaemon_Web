Hayaemon is a freeware audio player that can change the song's speed, frequency, and pitch at your will.
This software supports the most popular audio files formats as WAV, CDA, MP3, MP2, MP1, OGG, AIFF, AIF, APE, FLAC, M4A, MP4, AAC, etc.
